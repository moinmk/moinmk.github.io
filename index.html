vote mania

Vote Mania is a website in which the user can create a poll with certain question and options to vote for with a deadline. Option can be textual or graphical format. host can choose if the voter can choose one or multiple options in his poll. The users can vote to certain poll as of their choice, after voting the results will be shown to voter in the form of graphs. The hosted poll can be of any subject like sports, politics, entertainment etc.users can chhse from categories or can search for a specific poll. After poll ends person who hosted the poll and the users who have voted in the poll will get the notification about the poll result from where they can see the result.

technology stack: html, css, javascript, jquery, mysql
